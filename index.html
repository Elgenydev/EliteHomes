Get answers to the most common questions about buying, selling, and investing in real estate

You're ready to buy a home when you have stable income, a good credit score (typically 620+), a down payment saved (usually 10-20%), and you plan to stay in the area for at least 3-5 years.

Spring and early summer (March through June) are traditionally the best times to sell, as buyers are more active and homes show better. However, local market conditions are more important than the season.

Your offer should be based on comparable sales, the home's condition, current market conditions, and your budget. Our agents will provide a detailed CMA to help you make a competitive offer.

Closing costs typically range from 2-5% of the home's purchase price and include loan origination, appraisal, title insurance, attorney fees, and taxes. Both buyers and sellers have closing costs.
